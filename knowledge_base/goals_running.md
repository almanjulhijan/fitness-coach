# Running Goals

## Objective

Become a faster and more efficient runner while maintaining a sustainable lifestyle that includes strength training, climbing, work, travel, and social activities.

## Target Date

31 December 2026

## Primary Goals

### Aerobic Efficiency Goal

Run 5 km at 6:00/km pace while maintaining an average heart rate of 140 bpm or lower.

### Performance Goal

Run 5 km at 4:59/km pace or faster.

## Training Philosophy

- Prioritize consistency over intensity.
- Most runs should be easy aerobic runs.
- Avoid turning every run into a race effort.
- Increase weekly mileage gradually while minimizing injury risk.
- Running should remain sustainable alongside gym training, climbing, work, and life commitments.
- Optimize for long-term improvement rather than short-term PRs.

## Weekly Training Principles

- Run 3-5 times per week.
- Maintain at least one quality session per week (tempo, intervals, threshold, or hills).
- Keep approximately 80% of running volume in an easy aerobic zone.
- Target heart rate for easy runs: 135-145 bpm.
- Gradually increase weekly mileage over time while maintaining recovery quality.

## Habit Goals

- Maintain a minimum of 3 running sessions every week.
- Use short easy runs (e.g. 2 km morning runs) to build consistency and increase aerobic volume.
- Prioritize adherence and consistency over pace targets on easy days.

## Coach Instructions

When reviewing activities:

### Evaluate

- Progress toward 5K @ 6:00/km with HR ≤ 140 bpm.
- Progress toward 5K @ 4:59/km.
- Pace trends at comparable heart rates.
- Weekly mileage trends.
- Long run progression.
- Training consistency.

### Flag

- Excessive intensity.
- Too many hard sessions.
- Insufficient aerobic volume.
- Rapid mileage increases.
- Signs of overtraining.
- Poor recovery patterns.

### Recommend

- Weekly mileage adjustments.
- Easy vs hard run balance.
- Recovery improvements.
- Specific workouts that support current goals.
- Small habit changes that improve consistency.

## Success Criteria

- Consistent training throughout 2026.
- Sustainable aerobic development.
- Ability to run 5K at 6:00/km pace with average HR ≤ 140 bpm.
- Ability to run 5K at 4:59/km pace or faster.
- Reaching goals without recurring injuries.
